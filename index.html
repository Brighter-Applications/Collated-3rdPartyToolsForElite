The Commander's Compendium

A Definitive Guide to the Elite Dangerous Third-Party Tool Ecosystem

Introduction

Elite Dangerous's vast galaxy has inspired an extraordinary ecosystem of third-party tools created by the community. These applications transform the game from a solitary experience into a deeply interconnected, data-rich universe that many commanders consider essential to their gameplay.

About This Compendium

This guide consolidates the scattered landscape of Elite Dangerous third-party tools into one accessible reference. Each tool is categorized by function and explained in plain language, helping commanders discover the right applications for their playstyle and understand what each one actually does.

Part I covers currently operational tools, while Part II archives historical tools that are now defunct or superseded.

Part I: The Commander's Active Toolkit

Foundational Platforms

Inara

The definitive "one-stop shop" for most in-game needs. A comprehensive database for finding modules, materials, engineers, and tracking your synced Commander profile, fleet, and inventory.

EDSM

EDSM

The master of the unexplored black. EDSM (Elite Dangerous Star Map) is a massive, community-fed star map that functions as the primary record of galactic discovery, exploration logs, and expedition planning.

EDCodex

The index for the entire tool ecosystem. A community-driven database of all other third-party tools, websites, guides, and projects, making it the best place to discover new utilities.

Data Connectors

Elite Dangerous Market Connector

EDMC

A lightweight, essential application that reads your game journal and uploads anonymized data to the EDDN, Inara, and EDSM. Its power lies in a vast and robust plugin architecture.

EDDiscovery

EDD

An all-in-one, feature-heavy alternative to EDMC. A powerful dashboard that tracks your entire career history with integrated maps, voice synthesis, and over 50 information panels.

EDDI

EDDI

A unique companion application that enhances immersion through audio. It acts as an intelligent ship's computer, providing spoken feedback for in-game events. Integrates seamlessly with VoiceAttack.

Shipyard & Outfitting

EDSY

EDSY

Widely regarded as the most accurate and actively maintained ship-building tool. EDSY (Elite Dangerous Shipyard) uses precise mathematical models for calculating ship stats.

Coriolis

A very popular ship builder, known for its clean, intuitive, and aesthetically pleasing user interface. An excellent and highly usable tool for theory-crafting any build.

ED Ship Anatomy

A specialized tool providing detailed 3D models of every ship, illustrating the precise physical placement of all hardpoints and utility mounts. Vital for analyzing weapon convergence.

Galactic Navigation

Spansh's Elite Dangerous Tools

Spansh

The king of galactic route plotting. Features the essential Neutron Router, Fleet Carrier Router, Road to Riches planners, and powerful body/mining search tools.

ED Astro

A sophisticated suite for visualizing exploration data. Features galactic heatmaps, the Galactic Exploration Catalogue of unique points of interest, and a Travel History Video Creator.

Fleet Carrier Space

A community tool for managing and browsing fleet carriers. It collects data on carrier locations, manufactured commodities, deliveries, and routes, allowing owners to manage their assets.

Career-Specific Instruments

ED Odyssey Materials Helper

EDOMH

The modern standard for material tracking. This overlay tracks all materials and commodities for both Horizons and Odyssey engineering, highlighting what's needed for engineers or crafting.

I.N.T.R.A.

A specialized web utility and EDMC plugin for finding optimal system pairs for "massacre mission stacking," a lucrative PVE combat activity. It ranks the best systems based on live EDDN data.

ED Tools

A collection of web-based utilities that includes an invaluable Miner's Tool for finding mineral hotspots and high-sell stations, as well as tools for rare commodity trading and massacre missions.

TradeDangerous

TD

A powerful, command-line based application for dedicated traders. It features one of the most sophisticated trade run optimizers available, calculating complex, multi-hop routes to maximize profit.

BGS-Tally

An EDMC plugin for players who manipulate the Background Simulation (BGS). It meticulously tracks every action that influences minor factions, allowing squadrons to accurately measure their impact.

Anti-Xeno Initiative (AXI) Website & Wiki

The definitive resource for all aspects of Anti-Xeno (AX) combat. Contains meticulously tested ship builds, detailed guides on Thargoid behavior, and up-to-date intel on the Thargoid War.

Immersion & Interface

VoiceAttack & HCS VoicePacks

VA

VoiceAttack allows players to control their ship with spoken commands. HCS VoicePacks elevates this with professionally produced voice packs, creating a truly interactive and responsive ship AI.

EDHM UI

EDHM

A user-friendly interface for a powerful shader mod that allows for complete color customization of the in-game HUD. It offers far greater control than older methods.

Head Tracking (TrackIR, OpenTrack)

Allows players to control their in-game view by moving their head. OpenTrack is a popular free, open-source alternative to the commercial TrackIR, offering a huge immersion boost for non-VR players.

MFD Cougar Display

For dedicated sim-pit builders, this application extends the ship's instrumentation onto secondary monitors or Thrustmaster MFD panels, providing real-time graphical displays for in-game events.

Part II: The Digital Graveyard

Fallen Giants: Major Tools Now Offline

EDDB (Elite: Dangerous Database)

Status: Defunct. For many years, EDDB was one of the "big three" foundational websites. Renowned for its powerful search engines for commodities, modules, and trade routes. The site was shut down in April 2023.

ed-route.com

Status: Defunct. A web-based passenger mission route optimizer. It allowed users to input multiple passenger runs to compute the best multi-mission loops. The site is no longer active.

Superseded: Functional but Outdated

EDEngineer

Status: Superseded. A once-popular overlay for tracking engineering materials in Horizons. While still functional, it has been largely replaced by ED Odyssey Materials Helper (EDOMH), which is actively maintained and supports both Horizons and Odyssey materials.

Various HUD Color Changers (XML Method)

Status: Superseded. Older tools that modified an XML file to change HUD colors. This method is now obsolete and has been replaced by EDHM UI, which offers far greater control without undesirable side effects.

Confirmed Abandoned Projects

Elite Dangerous Controls Reference

Status: Abandoned. A web script that read a player's keybinds file and displayed a visual map of their controller layout. It has not been maintained for years.

mEDI's Elite Tools

Status: Abandoned. An early all-in-one desktop utility with a routes finder, trade optimizer, and shipyard. Development ceased many years ago and it is no longer updated.

Elite Trade Net (ETN)

Status: Abandoned. An HTML5 web tool designed to compute profit-maximizing trade routes for a specific ship and loadout. The project is no longer maintained.

ED ToolBox (EDTB)

Status: Abandoned. A feature-rich companion app that ran a local web server to provide real-time location data, maps, and logs. It has not been updated since 2017.

EliteBrainerous (EliteOCR)

Status: Abandoned. A standalone neural OCR and voice assistant engine. It was an early attempt at reading screen text for voice commands but development has long since stopped.
